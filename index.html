Products Categorise

Industrial Blade & Cutters

Industrial blades and cutters are specialized tools designed for cutting, slicing, and shaping various materials in manufacturing and production processes.

Spacers

Spacers are specialized components used in tire manufacturing to maintain precise spacing between tire layers during production.

Metal flap protectors

A metal flap protector for tires, often used in heavy-duty vehicles, is designed to shield the tire from debrisand damage.

Tyre serial number

A Tyre Serial Number, also known as a DOT number, is a unique code imprinted on the sidewall of a tyre for identification.

Welcome
Capital Industries

Capital Industries is equipped with the capabilities to provide a range of options for every unique die-cut project need. Made from various non-printed materials, die-cut components are cut into different shapes to solve specific problems such as sound dampening, EMI/RFI shielding, electrical insulation, thermal management, bonding, gap filling, sealing, air flow, and overheating.

At Capital Industries we provide multiple types of tooling processes including steel rule die, chemical etch die, rotary tooling, drag knife, CNC router, water jet, and Class-A (steel). Each method is available to determine which may be the best fit for your unique project.
